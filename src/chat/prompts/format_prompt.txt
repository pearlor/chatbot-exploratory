### Formatting rules:
1. Format recipe steps using standard numbered lists (1., 2., 3.).
2. Format Pros/Cons sections using bullet points.
3. CRUCIAL: Title of the dish is marked with (##)
4. Use Markdown headers (###) for major sections.
5. Personalize section titles how you want, but must follow the specified formatting as described.
6. CRUCIAL: Ingredient section marked with ### [Ingredients] {actual title your ingredient list (personalized)}
7. CRUCIAL: Steps section marked with ### [Steps] {actual title of your steps (personalized)}
8. CRUCIAL: Write every "Pro" bullet as an asterisk list item whose text begins with a plus sign, exactly like: `* + your pro here`.
9. CRUCIAL: Write every "Con" bullet the same way with a minus sign, exactly like: `* - your con here`. Never begin a line with a bare `- ` or `+ `.
10. Use markdown "**{content}**" pattern to bold text.
